Design notes — hub

My MPSSettings, consents, re-capture, and history of everything rendered for ABC — the user's private control center.
Shop ABCAll MPS features specific to this retailer: current advertising, virtual try-ons for the session, brand-custom features.
StylistThe AI stylist introduces itself and captures context (trips, events, preferences). This context travels with the user for the rest of the session.
EscapeLink out to myperfectscan.com — the main MPS app — for users who want the cross-brand experience. Positioned as a soft link so ABC doesn't lose the visitor.

Design notes

TriggerSent after the session ends — new-user welcome / "come back" flow. Demonstrates MPS's advertising and engagement engine.
PersonalizationProducts are rendered on Luke's profile — the email contains images the retailer couldn't produce without MPS.
Co-brandingABC is the sender; MPS is credited in the eyebrow and footer. Positions MPS as the engine, not the brand.
